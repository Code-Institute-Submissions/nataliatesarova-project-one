Laser Eye Surgery in the heart of London


Wave Eye Clinic is the premier provider of state-of-the-art laser eye surgery in London. Founded in 1995 by Mr John Walsh, a pioneer of LASIK vision correction in the UK. Mr Walsh undertook ophthalmic surgical training in Moorfields Eye Hospital, London and Bascom Palmer Eye Institute, USA. He has published extensively on LASIK and cataract surgery in peer-reviewed international journals, and given numerous regional, national and international lectures.

Wave Eye Clinic provides outstanding results by our highly trained surgeons using the latest technology. We ensure patients are well informed and fully supported in all stages of treatment and we have achieved thousands of successful patient results.
